fund it with testnet XLM, deposit into a vault, and withdraw your funds.

## What is VaultQuest?

VaultQuest is a **no-loss prize savings** application built on the Stellar
blockchain (Soroban smart contracts). You deposit funds into a pooled vault.
The yield generated by all deposits funds a weekly prize pool. One lucky
winner is chosen each round — but every depositor's principal remains fully
withdrawable at any time. **You can never lose your deposit.**

---

## Prerequisites

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- A **Stellar wallet** browser extension such as
  [Freighter](https://freighter.app) or
  [Albedo](https://albedo.link)
- A small amount of **testnet XLM** for transaction fees (see section 4)

## Connecting Your Wallet

1. Navigate to the VaultQuest app at `https://vaultquest.app`.
2. Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top-right corner of the page.

   ![Connect wallet button](https://placehold.co/600x120/EEE/31343C?text=Connect+Wallet+Button)

3. Select your preferred wallet from the list (Freighter, Albedo, etc.).

   ![Wallet selector modal](https://placehold.co/600x300/EEE/31343C?text=Wallet+Selector+Modal)

4. Approve the connection request in your wallet extension.
5. Once connected, your wallet address appears in the top-right corner. You
   are now ready to use VaultQuest.

   ![Connected wallet](https://placehold.co/600x80/EEE/31343C?text=Connected+Wallet+Address)

## Acquiring Testnet XLM

You need testnet XLM to pay for transaction fees (gas). These tokens have no
real value and are free to obtain.

### Via the Stellar Laboratory Friendbot

1. Open the [Stellar Laboratory](https://laboratory.stellar.org).
2. Paste your wallet address into the **Friendbot** section.
3. Click **Get testnet XLM**.
4. Within a few seconds, your wallet will receive 10,000 testnet XLM.
5. You can verify the balance in your wallet extension.

### Via the SDF Friendbot directly

1. Replace `YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS` in the URL below with your actual address:
   ```
   https://friendbot.stellar.org?addr=YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS
   ```
2. Open that URL in your browser.
3. A JSON response confirms the funds were sent.
4. Your wallet should now show a balance of 10,000 testnet XLM.

## The Dashboard

After connecting your wallet, you land on the **Dashboard** (`/app`). This
page shows:

- **Vault Overview** — key metrics about active vaults (TVL, APY,
  participant count)
- **Recent Winners** — the latest prize winners and amounts
- **Your Positions** — your active deposits across all vaults
- **Yield Calculator** — estimate your potential prize earnings

## Depositing Into a Vault

1. Navigate to the **Vaults** page via the top navigation bar.

   ![Vaults page](https://placehold.co/800x400/EEE/31343C?text=Vaults+Page)

2. Browse the available vaults. Each card shows:
   - Pool name and asset (e.g. "USDC Yield Pool")
   - Current APY (Annual Percentage Yield)
   - Total Value Locked (TVL)
   - Participant count

3. Click **Deposit** on the vault you want to join.

4. Enter the amount you wish to deposit.

   ![Deposit modal](https://placehold.co/600x400/EEE/31343C?text=Deposit+Modal)

5. Review the transaction summary:
   - Deposit amount
   - Estimated gas fee
   - Lockup period (if applicable)

6. Click **Confirm Deposit**.

7. Approve the transaction in your wallet extension.

   ![Wallet approval](https://placehold.co/400x300/EEE/31343C?text=Wallet+Approval+Prompt)

8. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the Stellar network (usually
   3–5 seconds).

9. A success toast appears in the bottom-right corner. Your deposit is now
   active and earning yield!

   ![Deposit success](https://placehold.co/400x80/EEE/31343C?text=Deposit+Success+Toast)

## Withdrawing From a Vault

1. Go to the **Account** page to see your active positions.

   ![Account page](https://placehold.co/800x400/EEE/31343C?text=Account+Page+with+Positions)

2. Find the vault position you want to withdraw from.
3. Click **Withdraw**.

4. Enter the amount. You can withdraw any portion of your deposit, up to your
   full balance.

   ![Withdrawal modal](https://placehold.co/600x400/EEE/31343C?text=Withdrawal+Modal)

5. Review the transaction details and click **Confirm Withdrawal**.

6. Approve the transaction in your wallet extension.

7. Once confirmed, the funds return to your wallet. The withdrawal appears in
   your **Activity** history.

## Troubleshooting

### Transaction stuck as "pending"

If a transaction stays in "pending" status for more than a few minutes:

1. Check the network status on the [Stellar Dashboard](https://status.stellar.org).
2. Refresh the page and check your **Activity** page for updates.
3. If the status does not change, try canceling the action and resubmitting.

### Insufficient XLM balance for fees

Each transaction requires a small XLM fee. If you see an insufficient balance
error:

1. Get more testnet XLM via the Friendbot (see [section 4](#acquiring-testnet-xlm)).
2. Wait for the new balance to appear in your wallet.
3. Retry the transaction.

### Wallet not connecting

If your wallet does not connect:

1. Ensure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked.
2. Check that you are on the correct Stellar network (testnet).
3. Refresh the page and try connecting again.
4. If the issue persists, try a different browser or wallet.
